# Refund Policy (Extended)

## Purpose

This policy defines how refunds are calculated, processed, and issued.

---

## Refund Eligibility

A refund is issued only when:

* The return is approved after inspection
* Or the order is confirmed defective or wrongfully shipped

---

## Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**:

* Credit Card → credited back to the same card
* Cash → store credit by default
* Other methods → original method when supported

---

## Refund Processing Timeline

* Inspection complete → refund initiated
* Target processing: **5–10 business days**
* Bank posting times vary and are outside platform control

---

## Refund Amount Rules

### Changed Mind / Size Issue

* Refund includes product price only
* Shipping fees are not refunded
* Return shipping may be charged depending on tier

### Defective / Wrong Item

* Refund includes:

  * Product price
  * Original shipping fee (if paid)
  * Return shipping cost

---

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may occur if:

* Item is missing tags
* Item is returned in poor condition
* Packaging is missing when required

Customer must be informed of the reason and amount difference.

---

## Cash Payments (Demo Rule)

* Default resolution is store credit
* Gold/VIP may request manual refund handling
* Cautious mode prefers store credit over cash refund

---

## Refund Reversals

If fraud or false claims are detected:

* Refunds may be reversed
* Account may be flagged
* Loyalty benefits may be removed

---

## Disputes and Chargebacks

* Refunds are paused during disputes
* Documentation is provided to the payment processor
* Final decision follows processor outcome
